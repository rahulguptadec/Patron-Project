Our Mission
Patron is all about connecting a person who needs help to a person who is ready to help. Consider the situation

A person (say X) who is going from A to B and is ready to help others. He / She may search into our web app and check if there is someone who wants help and if there is another person (say Y) who needs help and is going from C to D.Then, the role of patron is connect two people, so that they can go together.
How we started ?
It all started as a person project by a sophomore student. The main agenda is to keep the humanity alive : What we were known for and not what we are known for.
